## **How to Use This Document**

This document contains one prompt template for each of the 7 Master Blog Types defined in your SEO spreadsheet. Each prompt is designed to be copied and pasted directly into Marblism alongside the universal SH-Marblism-Instructions-v1.1.

The universal instructions (Sections 1-8) handle brand design, HTML structure, required components, citations, schema, voice, Joe's info, and mobile standards. These prompts handle the content-specific structure: what H2 sections to include, what trust blocks to build, what internal links to add, and what schema type to use.

**Workflow for Each New Blog Post**

1\. Open Marblism. Paste the universal SH-Marblism-Instructions-v1.1 as the base instruction set.

2\. Find the matching blog type prompt from this document (e.g., Comparison Post for a Tigard vs Tualatin article).

3\. Copy the prompt template. Fill in the bracketed variables: \[CITY\], \[PRIMARY KEYWORD\], \[TITLE\], etc.

4\. Paste the filled prompt into Marblism as the specific post instruction.

5\. Run Marblism. The output goes through Blog Project Category 5 (Compliance Pass) before publishing.

IMPORTANT: Every Marblism output must pass through the Blog Post Project (Category 5: Marblism Compliance Pass) before publishing to Lofty. Marblism drafts the content. The Blog Project enforces compliance.

**Variables Used in All Prompts**

\[TITLE\] \= Full blog post title as it will appear in the H1

\[PRIMARY KEYWORD\] \= Target keyword from the Blog Pipeline tab

\[SECONDARY KEYWORDS\] \= 2-3 related keywords, comma-separated

\[CITY\] \= City name (e.g., Tigard, Beaverton, Lake Oswego)

\[SLUG\] \= URL slug (e.g., tigard-vs-tualatin-which-suburb-fits)

\[PUBLISH DATE\] \= Target date in YYYY-MM-DD format

\[WORD COUNT\] \= Target from Blog Pipeline (e.g., 2500-3000)

\[FAQ TARGETS\] \= Specific People Also Ask questions from Blog Pipeline

# **Prompt 1: Community Spotlight (City Blog Post)**

**MAPS TO CATEGORY**

Community Page (primary) \+ Buying a Home (secondary)

**WHEN TO USE**

Any blog post focused on a specific Portland metro city: moving guides, city lifestyle posts, neighborhood overviews. This is the spoke content that links back to the city guide hub page.

CONTENT DUPLICATION RULE: This blog post must NOT duplicate the city guide at /living-in-\[city\]-oregon. The city guide is comprehensive (11+ sections). This blog post takes ONE specific angle on the city, such as a persona perspective, a seasonal angle, or a focused comparison.

**PROMPT TEMPLATE**

Write a blog post titled: \[TITLE\]

Primary keyword: \[PRIMARY KEYWORD\]

Secondary keywords: \[SECONDARY KEYWORDS\]

Target length: \[WORD COUNT\] words

Post slug: /blog/\[SLUG\]

Wrapper ID: post-\[SHORT-ID\]

Publish date: \[PUBLISH DATE\]

This is a Community Spotlight post about \[CITY\], Oregon. Follow all universal Marblism instructions for brand design, HTML structure, components, citations, schema, voice, and mobile.

USE THIS H2 STRUCTURE (adapt headings to match the specific angle):

H2 1: First Impressions of \[CITY\]

  \- What someone notices driving through for the first time

  \- Specific streets, landmarks, or vibes that define the character

  \- 1-2 named businesses or spots that set the tone

H2 2: Who Lives Here (and Why They Stay)

  \- Demographics and household types (cite city data or Census)

  \- What draws people: schools, commute, lifestyle, price point

  \- Name specific employers, school districts, parks

H2 3: The Housing Market Right Now

  \- Median home price (cite RMLS or Redfin, include date)

  \- Price range showing entry-level to premium

  \- Days on market, inventory trend

  \- Use a dark stat box (\#222222 background) for 3-4 key numbers

H2 4: Neighborhoods Worth Knowing

  \- 3-5 neighborhoods or areas within the city

  \- Each with a 2-3 sentence description and price context

  \- Name a restaurant, park, or landmark in each

H2 5: Getting Around

  \- Commute times to downtown Portland (cite Google Maps or TriMet)

  \- MAX, bus, highway access

  \- Walkability for errands and daily life

H2 6: Schools and Families

  \- School district name and 1-2 notable schools (cite GreatSchools or NCES)

  \- Family-friendly parks and activities

  \- Youth sports, library, community programs

H2 7: Local Favorites

  \- 5-8 named restaurants, coffee shops, breweries, or stores

  \- At least 2 that are locally owned (not chains)

  \- Seasonal events or farmers markets

H2 8: What to Watch Out For

  \- Honest caveats: traffic, noise, property tax rates, older housing stock, etc.

  \- This is a credibility section. Do not make it all positive.

H2 9: Joe's Take

  \- First person from Joe. Personal observation about the city.

  \- Reference a specific experience or showing.

  \- End with a soft CTA: invitation to explore, not a demand to call.

H2 10: FAQ (minimum 6 questions)

  \- Use these FAQ targets: \[FAQ TARGETS\]

  \- Add 2-3 more from actual People Also Ask results

  \- FAQ accordion with details/summary elements

  \- Must match FAQPage schema exactly

REQUIRED INTERNAL LINKS (check Editorial Linking Map for exact URLs):

  \- City guide hub: /living-in-\[city\]-oregon

  \- Market snapshot: /\[city\]\_snapshot

  \- IDX listings: /listing/single-family-home/\[city\]

  \- Market Update category: /blog?categoryId=57180

  \- At least 1 related blog post about a neighbor city

REQUIRED TRUST BLOCKS:

  \- Dark stat box with median price, population, school district, avg commute

  \- At least 1 Joe's Tip yellow box (gold left border, pale gold background)

  \- Exception box for when this city is NOT the right fit

SCHEMA: Article \+ BreadcrumbList \+ FAQPage (use @graph structure)

DESIGN ELEMENTS: Gold-underlined H2s, dark stat boxes, Joe's Tip yellow boxes, FAQ accordion, back-to-top links

**PROMPT TEMPLATE**

# **Prompt 5: Comparison Post (City vs City)**

**MAPS TO CATEGORY**

Community Page (primary) \+ Buying a Home (secondary)

**WHEN TO USE**

Head-to-head comparisons: Tigard vs Tualatin, Lake Oswego vs West Linn, condo vs townhome, FHA vs conventional. The most powerful spoke content for driving traffic to city guide hubs.

PREREQUISITE: Both compared city guides must be published (or scheduled) before this post goes live. If the hub does not exist yet, the comparison post's internal links have nowhere to go.

**PROMPT TEMPLATE**

Write a blog post titled: \[TITLE\]

Primary keyword: \[PRIMARY KEYWORD\]

Secondary keywords: \[SECONDARY KEYWORDS\]

Target length: \[WORD COUNT\] words (comparison posts are longer: 2500-3000)

Post slug: /blog/\[SLUG\]

Wrapper ID: post-\[SHORT-ID\]

Publish date: \[PUBLISH DATE\]

This is a Comparison Post. Follow all universal Marblism instructions.

USE THIS H2 STRUCTURE:

H2 1: Quick Comparison Table

  \- Side-by-side table: \[CITY A\] vs \[CITY B\]

  \- Rows: Median Price, Avg Sq Ft, School District, Commute to Portland, Walk Score, Property Tax Rate, Vibe/Character

  \- Wrap in overflow-x auto div

  \- Source every data point (RMLS, WalkScore, city website)

H2 2: The Housing Market Side by Side

  \- Median price comparison with source dates

  \- What you get for $500K in each city (specific examples)

  \- Days on market and inventory comparison

  \- Dark stat boxes for each city (two boxes, side by side on desktop, stacked on mobile)

H2 3: Schools and Families

  \- School district comparison with ratings

  \- Family amenities: parks, programs, youth sports

  \- Named schools with GreatSchools ratings

H2 4: Commute, Transit, and Getting Around

  \- Drive times to downtown Portland from each

  \- MAX/TriMet access differences

  \- Highway proximity (I-5, 217, 26, 205\)

H2 5: Lifestyle and Local Character

  \- What daily life feels like in each (specific, not generic)

  \- Named restaurants, coffee shops, grocery stores in each

  \- Weekend activities and seasonal events

H2 6: Best For... (Decision Framework)

  \- Gold callout: '\[CITY A\] is the better fit if you...'

  \- Gold callout: '\[CITY B\] is the better fit if you...'

  \- Name specific personas: first-time buyer on a budget, family with teens, downsizer, remote worker

H2 7: Joe's Take

  \- First person. Which city does Joe recommend for which buyer?

  \- Reference showing experience in both cities.

  \- CTA: 'Want to see both? I can set up showings in \[CITY A\] and \[CITY B\] the same day.'

  \- Link to /contact

H2 8: FAQ (minimum 6 questions)

  \- Use these FAQ targets: \[FAQ TARGETS\]

  \- Include: 'Is \[CITY A\] or \[CITY B\] better for families?'

  \- Include: 'Which is more affordable, \[CITY A\] or \[CITY B\]?'

REQUIRED INTERNAL LINKS (BOTH cities):

  \- City guide: /living-in-\[city-a\]-oregon AND /living-in-\[city-b\]-oregon

  \- Market snapshots for both cities

  \- IDX listings for both cities

  \- Market Update category: /blog?categoryId=57180

  \- Related comparison posts if they exist
